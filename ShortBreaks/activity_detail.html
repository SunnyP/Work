Junior Activity Summer Scheme

TEXT ABOUT ACTIVITY

Offering education and leisure activities away from home for children (3 – 9 yrs) with autism and other disabilities.

image

image

Support provided: Pre-booked activities

Onsite activities include arts and crafts, cooking, sensory room, soft play room, sensory games, sensory activities and physical play.

Offsite activities include swimming, climbing and trampolining.

Additional languages: Urdu

 

Timing: Weekdays 10:00 – 15:00 during school holidays

Location: Richmond Hill School, Sunridge Ave, Luton LU2 7JL

Referral types: Self-Referral

Capacity: 25

Price: £16.50

TEXT ABOUT PROVIDER

We have a dedicated and highly experienced leadership team who plan and run the sessions in accordance with the likes and dislikes of the children. We obtain this information through a registration form together with formal and informal evaluations.
